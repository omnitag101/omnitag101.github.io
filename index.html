Core Features

One Keychain.

Feature 01

Door Monitor

Hang OmniTag on any door handle and it becomes an invisible guardian. The moment your door swings open, your phone receives an instant alert. Perfect for hostel rooms, hotel stays, and study spaces — sleep soundly knowing you'll be notified the second anyone enters.

Motion Detection Instant Phone Alert No Setup Needed
Feature 02

Forget-Me-Not

Attach OmniTag to your bag, laptop case, or travel luggage. If you walk beyond a defined distance without it, your phone vibrates with an immediate proximity alert. Never leave a café without your MacBook again. Your belongings follow your awareness — not the other way around.

BLE Proximity Custom Range Vibration Alert
Feature 03

Remote Shutter

Press the physical shutter button on OmniTag and capture the perfect shot from anywhere in the frame — no strangers, no awkward timers, no sprint-back blur. Prop your phone, step into the scene, and click. Designed for solo travelers, content creators, and anyone who deserves to be in their own memories.

Physical Button 10m+ Range iOS & Android
Feature 04

Smart LED Light

A powerful built-in emergency LED that activates instantly — from late-night key searches to power cuts, early morning journeys, and campus walks. The same blue indicator you see on the device doubles as a status light, keeping you informed of connectivity at a glance.

Emergency Light Status Indicator Instant Trigger
